We want to help our Photographers obtain maximum exposure. To achieve this, we've developed a fair and dynamic system that will allow them a chance to be promoted on our home page.

Each and every Monday, we will select a handful of the best images that have been submitted to our weekly Assignments category. After selection, the images will be added to the Featured Media section. Within this special section, members have the chance to vote for their favourite image. Once our selections are placed in the Featured Media section, the image that is the most liked at the end of the week will be automatically promoted as the most prominent home page image for the following weekly period. This image remains fixed for an entire weekly period providing the Photographer with a week of front-line promotion. That's pretty cool right? Also, during the voting phase, the image that has the highest current like count will be displayed as the second most prominent home page image. This image can change dynamically as votes pour in during the week. :)

So please... Support our Photographers! Check out the Featured Media page (From footer menu: Hover over Photos and select Featured) and click the like button on your favourite image! :)
